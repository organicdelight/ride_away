Recovery Service

Ride Away Towing & Logistics specializes in the transport of vehicles that have been in an accident or damaged. Ride Away Towing & Logistics uses special-equipped vehicles such as tow trucks to transport vehicles.

  • Recovery services: Ride Away Towing service and logistics are necessary to transport your vehicle to a garage or tire shop if it develops mechanical issues. They may also be utilized for off-road driving safety and lockout assistance if keys get locked inside your car accidentally.

  • Illegal parking: Illegal parking is the act of parking your vehicle in an area not authorized or allowed by law. This could include blocking entrances and fire hydrants, as well as using handicapped spots without valid permits. Illegal parking can lead to traffic congestion, and inconvenience for pedestrians and other drivers alike. Ride Away towing service can remove your vehicle and impound it until you pay the required fees.
